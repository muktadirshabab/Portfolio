Early Life & Growth

From school days to creating design and film work, I’ve always been fascinated by how ideas can move people. I learned to see design not just as beauty, but as conversation.

Inspiration

My inspiration comes from my surroundings, emotions, and the people who believe in me — especially my aunt, my teacher Rony Kumar Nath, and Arifa. They remind me that vision matters most when it’s shared.

Future Vision
